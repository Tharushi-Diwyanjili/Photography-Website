Who Am I?

I'm Malcolm Lismore, a passionate freelance photographer capturing the untamed beauty of Scotland's North West coast. With a lens always focused on the natural world, my love for the rugged Scottish landscape, its diverse wildlife, and coastal birds is reflected in every image I create.



Why Choose Me?


Expertise

Having immersed myself in photography for years, I bring a wealth of experience and a keen eye for detail to every project. From the grandeur of landscapes to the delicate expressions of wildlife, my goal is to freeze moments in time that tell compelling stories.

Versatility

While my heart belongs to nature, I'm not limited to it. I offer my lens for hire in diverse realms, including weddings, portraits, and special events. Every occasion is a canvas for me to paint a visual masterpiece.

Distinctive Style

What sets me apart is not just the click of a shutter, but the artistry behind each photograph. I strive for a style that is uniquely mine – a blend of authenticity, emotion, and a touch of the extraordinary.

Passion and Dedication

Photography is not just a profession for me; it's a lifelong passion. I approach each project with dedication, ensuring that the essence of the moment is preserved in its purest form.

Tailored Approach

Understanding the unique nature of every event, I work closely with clients to tailor my services to their vision. Your story deserves to be told in a way that is distinctly yours.

Memories that Last

I believe in creating not just photographs but enduring memories. Whether it's a wedding, a portrait session, or a special event, my commitment is to deliver images that evoke emotions and stand the test of time.
